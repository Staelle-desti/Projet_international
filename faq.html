❓ FAQ

How do I apply for Visale?
You can apply for Visale online on the Visale website. Make sure you have all the required documents.
What is CAF and how do I apply?
CAF (Caisse d'Allocations Familiales) provides financial assistance for housing. You can apply on the CAF website.
Where can I find student residences?
You can find student residences through CROUS or rental platforms like SeLoger.
